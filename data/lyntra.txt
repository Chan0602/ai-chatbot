Project: Lyntra AI Study Planner

Type: AI-powered Product / EdTech / UX Design

Description:
An AI-powered study planning tool designed to help students organize their learning, manage time effectively, and stay on track with academic goals.

Users:
- Students managing multiple courses and deadlines
- Users who struggle with time management and planning
- Individuals seeking personalized study support

Problem:
- Students often lack structured planning for studying
- Difficulty prioritizing tasks across multiple courses
- Low motivation and inconsistency in study habits
- Existing tools are static and not personalized

My Role:
Product Designer (UX + AI Interaction Design)

Actions:
- Designed an AI-driven study planning experience
- Created user flows for generating personalized study plans
- Defined interaction patterns between user and AI assistant
- Structured task breakdown and scheduling logic
- Designed interfaces for tracking progress and adjusting plans
- Focused on reducing cognitive load for users

Key Features:
- AI-generated personalized study plans
- Dynamic task breakdown based on user goals
- Progress tracking and adaptive scheduling
- Conversational interaction with AI assistant
- Smart prioritization of tasks

AI Component:
- AI interprets user goals and generates structured plans
- Adapts recommendations based on user progress
- Provides guidance and feedback through interaction

Impact:
- Improved users’ ability to manage study schedules
- Reduced overwhelm in planning complex workloads
- Increased consistency in study habits
- Enhanced personalization compared to static planners

Skills:
- Product Design
- AI Interaction Design
- UX Design
- User Flow Design
- Human-AI Interaction

Tools:
Figma, Prototyping Tools

Keywords:
AI product, study planner, edtech, personalization, task management, AI assistant, productivity, learning tools

Summary:
I designed an AI-powered study planner that helps students create personalized learning plans, manage tasks, and stay consistent through adaptive AI guidance.
