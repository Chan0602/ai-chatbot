Project: MAPWA Practicum Platform Redesign

Type: Information System / Educational Platform / UX Design

Description:
A redesign of the MAPWA (Practicum Web App) system used by over 200 MA-LIS students at UW–Madison to manage practicum opportunities, applications, and onboarding.

Users:
- Graduate students (MA-LIS program)
- Practicum coordinators
- Site supervisors

Problem:
- Students struggled to navigate practicum requirements and opportunities
- Information was scattered across different pages and documents
- The onboarding and application process lacked clarity
- Limited visibility into partner organizations and project details

My Role:
UX Designer + Researcher

Research Methods:
- Contextual inquiry
- User interviews with students
- Workflow analysis of practicum process
- Stakeholder feedback (program coordinators)

Actions:
- Mapped the end-to-end practicum journey for students
- Identified pain points in onboarding, application, and tracking
- Redesigned information architecture to centralize key content
- Created a clearer navigation system for opportunities and requirements
- Designed structured partner/project listings for easier comparison
- Improved onboarding flow for new users

Key Features / Improvements:
- Centralized practicum information in one platform
- Structured partner directory with clear project details
- Improved navigation for browsing opportunities
- Streamlined onboarding and application workflow
- Clearer status tracking for practicum progress

Impact:
- Improved clarity for 200+ students navigating practicum requirements
- Reduced confusion in application and onboarding process
- Increased efficiency in finding and comparing opportunities
- Enhanced overall user experience of the MAPWA system

Skills:
- UX Research
- Information Architecture
- System Design
- User Journey Mapping
- Interaction Design

Tools:
Figma, Research Documentation Tools

Keywords:
education platform, information system, onboarding, workflow design, information architecture, student experience, dashboard, platform redesign

Summary:
I redesigned the MAPWA practicum platform to improve how students discover opportunities, understand requirements, and navigate the onboarding process, impacting over 200 users.
